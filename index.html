RECENT WORK

NASA

Government Agency Redesign

CHALLENGE

The NASA website has a wealth of information. There are many articles of everything having to do with the history of NASA and their explorations. Through user reviews we found that the main reason people visit the site is to view the galleries. After reviewing the homepage we found that the presentation of the articles can be overwhelming. The navigation bar has too many links, some related to others and can be consolidated. We want to simplify the site so that it is easier for viewers to digest the large amount of information.

SOLUTION

We decided to highlight the next upcoming event on the homepage with an eye catching photo. The call to action to find out more information about the event will include a countdown for urgency. We condensed the navigation bar to just four links. Each link would also open up to a sub menu, while dimming the background and unchosen links. This also serves as a visual breadcrumb trail. We also would like to see icons next to the nav links for a more modern look that is easier to identify.
